Why Semantics?

Using <div> for everything is a "Junior" mistake. A "Topper" uses semantic tags to give meaning to the structure. This improves SEO and Accessibility (A11y).

Did you know?

Screen readers use these tags to help visually impaired users navigate your site efficiently.

Article Element

An <article> is a self-contained composition. It should make sense even if it's pulled out of the page (like a blog post, news story, or forum post).

Written by Sonam Narula
Published on: March 18, 2026

Section vs Article

Use <section> for grouping related content. It usually has a heading.
